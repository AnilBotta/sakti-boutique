# 01 — Product Brief

## Identity
Sakti Boutique is a **premium direct-to-consumer ethnic fashion boutique**. The experience must feel luxurious, editorial, festive, and culturally rich — never discount-store, never generic.

## Audience
- **Women** — primary audience, broadest catalog
- **Men** — focused ethnic essentials
- **Kids** — curated festive and everyday ethnicwear

## Brand Positioning
- Premium boutique, not mass-market marketplace
- Festive and cultural storytelling at the core
- Editorial presentation over transactional grids
- High-conversion without sacrificing elegance
