## Intent modifiers
- "near me" (local US markets with Indian diaspora: Jersey City, Edison, Houston, Dallas, Chicago, Bay Area, Atlanta, Seattle)
- "for wedding guest"
- "for bride"
- "for mother of the bride"
- "plus size"
- "in [color]" (ivory, pastel, emerald, rani pink, mustard, wine, midnight blue, rose gold)
- "with stitched blouse" (important Sakthi differentiator)

## Anti-keywords (avoid)
- "cheap" · "discount" · "clearance" · "sale" · "70% off" — dilute premium positioning
- "traditional" as a standalone — too generic; pair with fabric/craft specifics
- "Indian dress" — use the specific garment name
